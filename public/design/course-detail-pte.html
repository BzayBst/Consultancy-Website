PTE Academic

🎯 Score 50 – 79+ ⏱ 1–3 Months 🏫 HASU Language Institute

Master PTE Academic with HASU's focused computer-based test preparation. Our trainers teach strategies for Speaking, Writing, Reading, and Listening with AI-scoring practice tools and real exam simulations.

PTE is widely accepted for Australia, UK, and New Zealand study visas and university admissions. Our program emphasizes timed practice, template building, and pronunciation clarity for high speaking scores.

Flexible batches and weekend intensives are available at our Bhairahawa campus with free study materials and weekly mock tests included in every enrollment.

What You Will Learn

  • Speaking & reading aloud with fluency drills
  • Summarize written text and spoken lectures
  • Essay writing and summarize written text tasks
  • Describe image and retell lecture practice
  • Computer-based mock tests with score reports
