About The Company

Your Trusted Partner in Global Education

Established in 2013 and officially registered in 2015, HASU International Educational Pvt. Ltd. is the best educational consultancy in Bhairahawa. We guide Nepali students to higher education opportunities in Japan, Australia, Canada, the US, UK, and New Zealand through comprehensive counseling and application support.

We specialize in Japanese language prep (NAT, JLPT, J-TEST), English exams (IELTS, PTE), and full visa processing — ensuring a smooth journey from planning to placement.
