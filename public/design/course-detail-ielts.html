🇬🇧 IELTS Preparation

IELTS Preparation

🎯 Band 6.0 – 8.0+ ⏱ 2–4 Months 🏫 HASU Language Institute

Achieve your target IELTS band with HASU's structured preparation program. Our IDP-certified trainers deliver expert coaching across Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking for both Academic and General Training tests.

We focus on proven strategies, timed mock tests, and personalized feedback on writing and speaking — the sections where most Nepali students need the most support. Small batch sizes ensure individual attention every week.

Whether you need IELTS for UK, Australia, Canada, or university admissions worldwide, HASU helps you build confidence and score consistently on exam day.

What You Will Learn

  • Listening techniques for accents and fast speech
  • Reading strategies for True/False/Not Given questions
  • Task 1 & Task 2 writing templates and band descriptors
  • Speaking fluency, pronunciation, and cue card practice
  • Full-length mock tests with detailed score analysis
