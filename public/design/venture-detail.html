About This Venture

Your Gateway to World-Class Education

HASU Educational Consultancy is the flagship venture of the HASU family. Founded in Bhairahawa, we have spent over a decade helping Nepali students secure admissions, scholarships, and visas for institutions across the globe.

We don't just process paperwork — we build personalized pathways from your first counseling session through pre-departure orientation, ensuring you arrive abroad confident and prepared.

  • Official partnerships with universities in 10+ countries
  • End-to-end visa documentation and interview prep
  • Scholarship and financial aid guidance
  • Pre-departure briefing and alumni support network

How It Works

Your Journey With HASU

A clear, step-by-step process from first visit to boarding your flight abroad.

01

Free Counseling

02
