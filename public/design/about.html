Our Story

How HASU Began Its Journey

Founded in 2013 and officially registered in 2015, HASU International Educational Pvt. Ltd. started with a single mission: make world-class education accessible to every Nepali student. From a small office in Bhairahawa, we have grown into one of the most trusted educational consultancies in the region.

We specialize in guiding students to top universities in Japan, Australia, Canada, the USA, UK, and New Zealand — providing comprehensive support from counseling and application to visa processing and pre-departure orientation.

2013
Founded by Educational Visionaries

HASU was established with a vision to bridge the gap between Nepali students and global universities.

2015
Officially Registered & Expanded

Received official government registration and expanded services to include Japanese language training.

2018
Launched HASU Language Institute

Opened a dedicated language training center for NAT, JLPT, J-TEST, IELTS, and PTE preparation.

2024
5,000+ Students Successfully Placed

Crossed the milestone of placing over five thousand students in universities across 10+ countries.
